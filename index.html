Introduction

Sevvy is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("Sevvy").

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The app stores only local gameplay data (game progress, scores, and settings) on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Nothing you do in Sevvy is sent to us. Your data stays on your device, always.

No Account Required

Sevvy does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Third-Party Services

Sevvy uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain device information (such as advertising ID, IP address, and device type) to provide relevant ads. Where required by law, we collect your consent before personalized ads are shown.

Please see Google's Privacy Policy for more details.

Children's Privacy

Sevvy does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. All data remains local to your device.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data relies on your device's built-in protections. We recommend using your device's security features (PIN, password, or biometric lock).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page. Continued use of Sevvy after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
